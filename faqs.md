# Frequently Asked Questions
* Why ext4 (and f2fs) and not [insert filesystem here]?

Most filesystems sugessted are either:
1. Too old (e.g. ext2)
2. Unsupported
3. Have their own snapshot system (btrfs or xfs)

* Why "ObsidianOS" as the name?

It sounds cool and this distribution is made to be as solid as an obsidian (in Minecraft, of course.).
